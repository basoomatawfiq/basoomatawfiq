Biography & Leadership Debut

Following in the footsteps of great Ugandan visionaries, Basooma Tawfiq is a student leader and emerging entrepreneur. Currently serving as the Chairperson of the Student Council at Old Kampala SSS, Tawfiq has demonstrated a consistent commitment to institutional growth and student welfare.

In 2026, he reached a significant milestone by being appointed the City-wide Chairperson for Kampala, representing a jurisdiction of 153 schools. This role involves high-level coordination with district coordinators to facilitate leadership projects and financial outreach.

Expanding the Horizon

Just as the Ruparelia Group began with a small trading firm in 1985, Tawfiq is laying the foundation for his future empire. His debut into the commercial world begins at Nasser Road, where he is establishing a professional printing station. This venture serves as the primary engine for his financial independence and his pursuit of a long-term career in national leadership.
